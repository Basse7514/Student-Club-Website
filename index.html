Upcoming Events

We have a variety of events lined up for you. Check out the ones that interest you:

Track and Field Meet

Unleash your speed and strength in the Eagles Athletics Challenge! Featuring events like sprints, long jump, relay races, and more, this competition is perfect for athletes of all levels. Whether you're chasing a personal best or cheering on your peers, this event promises excitement and energy for everyone.

CrossFit Challenge

Test your endurance, strength, and agility in the ultimate fitness showdown: the Limit Breaker Battle! This event brings together students in a series of intense workouts designed to push your limits. Compete individually or as a team and prove you're the fittest on campus.

Marathon/Walkathon

Join us for the Eagles Charity Run, a thrilling run through scenic routes that combines endurance and community spirit. Whether you’re aiming to break records or simply enjoy the journey, this marathon welcomes runners of all skill levels. Lace up and be part of something unforgettable!
